Description
===========
  The Feedback sample shows Force Feedback via devices that support it.

Path
====

  Source:     DXSDK\Samples\VB.NET\DirectInput\Feedback
  Executable: DXSDK\Samples\VB.NET\DirectInput\Bin

User's Guide
============
  Observe how the forces feel when you move and twist the stick, 
  rotate the throttle wheel, and press buttons in various combinations.
